Frequently Asked Questions


Why do I need to register on the site?
Establishing an online account with Libralize assures you that your purchasing information is secure, confidential and accessible to you.
What if I can't find what I'm looking for?
The best way to locate a product in the online bookstore is to use the Product Search functions located in the top area of the user-panel after login.
How will Libralize protect my anonymity?
Confidentiality is important to us. Libralize respects your anonymity. We do not share our users list with any organization outside of Libralize.
What is the Book Aid Fund?
Libralize gives recovery resources to individuals and agencies in need through the Libralize Book Aid Program.
Searching for products within the bookstore
To optimize your search results, we suggest using the branch and semester filter dropdowns.
How can I contact you to enquire about my online order?
Please e-mail "libralizeofficial@gmail.com" with your questions.
